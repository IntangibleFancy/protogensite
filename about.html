About Me

Welcome to the world of distinguished facial hair! I'm Matt Bruning, a internationally recognized mustache historian, stylist, and curator with over fifteen years dedicated to the art and science of upper lip excellence. As a certified member of the World Mustache and Beard Championships Committee and former President of the American Mustache Renaissance Society, I've had the privilege of judging competitions across four continents and mentoring thousands of aspiring mustache enthusiasts. My expertise spans from the classic Handlebar and Walrus styles to the more avant-garde Imperial and Hungarian techniques. I hold advanced certifications from both the London Institute of Facial Hair Studies and the prestigious Bavarian Academy of Traditional Grooming Arts, where I completed my Master's thesis on "The Sociocultural Impact of the Mustache in 19th Century European Aristocracy." When I'm not researching historical grooming practices or consulting for Hollywood productions, you can find me perfecting new waxing formulations or curating rare mustache artifacts for my private collection.
